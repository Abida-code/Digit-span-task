The Digit span memory task consists of following two subtasks:

  • Forward task: This task consists of Random numbers that will appear on Screen and will disappear and you will enter sequence of random numbers on the screen. For example, if the digit appeared on screen is 6535 you will answer on screen.
  • Backward task: This task consists of Random numbers that will appear on Screen and will disappear and you will enter backward sequence of random numbers on the screen. For example, if the digit appeared on is 6535 you will answer on screen.
